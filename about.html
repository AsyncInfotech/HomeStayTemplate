About Demo Homestay

Discover our story, values, and the people who make your stay unforgettable

Our Story

Demo Homestay was born from a simple dream - to share the breathtaking beauty of our mountain homeland with travelers from around the world. What started as a small family initiative in 2010 has grown into a beloved destination for those seeking authentic experiences.

Our homestay is named after the beautiful Demos that bloom in our gardens each spring, symbolizing strength, beauty, and harmony with nature. These values are at the heart of everything we do.

Over the years, we've welcomed guests from over 50 countries, each bringing their own stories and leaving with memories that last a lifetime. We believe that travel is not just about seeing new places, but about connecting with people and cultures.

Our Values

Authentic Hospitality

We treat every guest as family, providing warm, genuine hospitality that reflects our local culture and traditions.

Sustainability

We're committed to protecting our environment through eco-friendly practices and supporting our local community.

Community Connection

We believe in creating meaningful connections between our guests and the local community, fostering cultural exchange.

Quality Experience

From comfortable accommodations to memorable activities, we ensure every aspect of your stay exceeds expectations.
