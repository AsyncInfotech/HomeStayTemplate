Frequently Asked Questions

How far is the homestay from the nearest airport?
We are approximately 2 hours drive from the nearest airport. We can arrange transportation for guests upon request.
What meals are included in the stay?
We offer traditional breakfast and dinner featuring local, organic ingredients. Lunch can be arranged for day-long activities.
Is Wi-Fi available throughout the property?
Yes, we provide complimentary Wi-Fi in all rooms and common areas, though speeds may vary due to our mountain location.
What is the best time to visit?
The best time to visit is during spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) for clear mountain views and pleasant weather.
Do you arrange guided tours and activities?
Yes, we offer various guided activities including hiking, cultural tours, cooking classes, and photography tours with local experts.
What should I pack for my stay?
Pack comfortable hiking shoes, warm layers for evenings, sun protection, and a camera for the beautiful scenery!
